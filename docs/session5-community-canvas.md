## Construction Steps

### Phase 1: Preparation & Safety (Day 1)

**⚠️ Safety Requirements**
- Welded metal can have sharp edges → Gloves mandatory
- Rust flakes → Wear goggles and mask when scrubbing
- Primer fumes → Paint in ventilated area, wear mask

**Steps:**
1. **Drum cleaning:** Scrub drums with wire brush to remove loose rust
2. **Tire cleaning:** Wash tires with soap, scrub dirt from treads
3. **Paint sorting:** Group leftover paints by color/compatibility
4. **Workspace prep:** Cover ground with plastic sheeting
5. **Community briefing:** Assign roles (paint team, assembly team, safety monitor)

---

### Phase 2: Priming (Day 1)

**Steps:**
1. Apply primer to all exposed drum surfaces (2-3 thin coats)
2. Allow each coat to dry 30 minutes before next
3. Prime tire inner and outer surfaces
4. **Quality check:** Ensure no bare metal or rust visible after primer

---

### Phase 3: Design & Painting (Day 2)

**Steps:**

**Animal Faces:**
- Drum 1: Paint body bright yellow → Add lion mane (orange strokes) → Paint ears and happy smiling face
- Drum 2: Paint body soft gray → Add elephant trunk and ears → Paint cheerful expression
- Drum 3: Paint body orange with black stripes → Add whiskers and bright tiger face

**Tire Decoration:**
- Base tire: Paint green with vine patterns → Add tropical flower shapes (pink, yellow, white)
- Top tire: Paint as flower petals radiating outward → Use this rim as a planter rim

**Details:**
- Add "PUT WASTE HERE" text in friendly fonts above each animal's mouth
- Paint small paw prints around drum bases

---

### Phase 4: Assembly (Day 2)

**Steps:**
1. **Anchoring base:** Position base tire (green tier) on concrete, mark locations
2. **Drill holes** through tire and concrete for bolt anchoring
3. **Stack drums:** Place drum 1 (lion) on base tire, position carefully
4. **Secure drums:** Drill through drum bottom into tire, bolt together (use 4 bolts per drum for stability)
5. **Repeat** for drums 2 and 3 in tiered arrangement
6. **Top tire planter:** Painted flower tire screwed onto top drum rim — add small plant later

---

### Phase 5: Finishing & Sealing (Day 3)

**Steps:**
1. Inspect all paintwork, touch up scratches or spots
2. Apply clear sealant spray to all painted surfaces (2-3 coats)
3. Allow sealant to cure 24 hours
4. Line each drum interior with plastic bag liners before first use
5. Add small succulent plant to top tire planter
6. Install "PROUDLY BY COMMUNITY RESIDENTS" plaque

---

### Phase 6: Launch (Day 4)

**Community Event:**
- Organized inauguration with children
- "First toss" ceremony where kids throw ceremonial waste
- Photo session for community X post
- Maintenance volunteers sign up for weekly bin checks
